4.4 (1.23k)

Chicken Curry Rice is a flavorful dish rich in protein and spices. Easy to make, nutritious, perfect for meal prep, and satisfying. Loved by all, it blends taste with health beautifully.

45-60 minutes

Steps:
  • Marinate chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili, and salt
  • Heat oil and sauté onions until golden brown
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till fragrant
  • Mix in tomatoes and cook until soft and pulpy
  • Add spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala
  • Stir in marinated chicken and cook until sealed
  • Pour in water and simmer until chicken is tender
  • Cook rice separately with a pinch of salt
  • Serve hot chicken curry over freshly steamed rice
  • Garnish with coriander leaves and a dash of lemon juice

Recipe

Mushroom Curry
Medium

4.6 (980)

Mushroom curry is a delicious vegetarian dish packed with earthy flavors, aromatic spices, and a creamy texture. It’s easy to cook, protein-rich, and perfect for lunch or dinner meals.

Steps:
  • Clean and slice mushrooms evenly
  • Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions till golden
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears
  • Mix in chopped tomatoes and cook until soft
  • Stir in spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala
  • Add mushrooms and cook until they release water
  • Pour in a little water or coconut milk for a creamy base
  • Let the curry simmer for 10–15 minutes
  • Adjust salt and spice levels to taste
  • Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or roti

Recipe

Sprouts Salad
Easy

4.8 (2.1k)

Sprouts Salad is a healthy, crunchy, and refreshing dish packed with proteins and nutrients. It's quick to make, perfect for weight loss, and ideal as a light lunch or snack.

Steps:
  • Rinse and soak green gram (moong) sprouts overnight
  • Steam or lightly boil sprouts until slightly tender (optional)
  • Chop onions, tomatoes, cucumbers, and green chilies finely
  • In a bowl, combine sprouts with all chopped veggies
  • Add lemon juice and sprinkle salt and chaat masala
  • Mix in coriander leaves and mint for extra freshness
  • Add a dash of pepper and cumin powder for flavor
  • Toss everything gently and chill for 10 minutes
  • Optionally top with roasted peanuts or pomegranate
  • Serve cold for a crunchy, nutritious treat
